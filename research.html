Research Focus

Ultrasonic Evaluation

Our research focuses on using ultrasonic techniques to evaluate the structural integrity and properties of advanced materials. This work includes nondestructive evaluation of defects, material characterization, and wave propagation analysis.

Beam Modelling

Our beam modelling research aims to simulate wave propagation in complex media. This work supports the development of improved nondestructive testing techniques and enhances our understanding of material behavior under stress.

Polymer Additive Manufacturing

We explore polymer-based additive manufacturing methods to create intricate and lightweight structures. Our studies focus on optimizing process parameters and enhancing material properties through innovative techniques.

Metal Additive Manufacturing

Our work in metal additive manufacturing focuses on process development for materials like titanium and aluminum alloys. We aim to enhance mechanical properties and ensure structural reliability through advanced process monitoring and control.
