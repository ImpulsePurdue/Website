Welcome to the IMPULSE Lab

We are the IMPULSE research lab in the School of Mechanical Engineering at Purdue University. Our research focuses on ultrasonic evaluation, additive manufacturing, and advanced material characterization. Through cutting-edge experiments and innovative modeling, we strive to advance the understanding and application of these technologies to solve real-world challenges.
